makerere library sections

  • reference and circulation section. to register library users,handle book reservations and issue library rules and regulations

  • book bank section; maintains a database of acquired books and distrubutes the new processed books to respective units among others
  • the international development agency(IDA); this house reference materials ie dictionaries, encyclopedias, year books an text books for undergraduate studies
  • techinical service section ; it does the cataloguing and classification and its out of bound to library users
  • microfilming and photographic section, digitisation,book bank and bindery
